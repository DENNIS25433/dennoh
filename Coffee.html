    A Passion for Perfect Coffee

    Founded in 2025, Karis Coffee Pub began as a small dream to create a community space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

    We source our beans directly from ethical farms around the world and roast them in-house to bring out their unique flavors. Every cup is crafted with care by our skilled baristas.
