Our Story

Crafting Coffee Experiences Since 2024

KARIS COFFEE PUB was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a space where people could connect over beautifully crafted beverages. Our founder, a third-generation coffee roaster, wanted to bring specialty coffee to the community in an approachable yet elevated way.

We source our beans directly from ethical farms around the world, roast them in small batches to highlight their unique characteristics, and prepare each drink with care and precision.
